# Puffpaw App

Earning tokens requires you to log in and connect your Smart Vape to the Puffpaw App via Bluetooth. Your phone MUST be connected to the internet to ensure each puff is recorded on-chain. This is essential for earning your token rewards.&#x20;

The Puffpaw App will be listed on both IOS App Store and Google App Store.

App Download Instructions: [guide.puffpaw.xyz](https://guide.puffpaw.xyz/)
