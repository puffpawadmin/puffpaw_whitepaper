# Organic E-liquid

For example, one of our non-nicotine pods is made primarily with tea extracts as the main ingredient.

Tea extracts, especially catechins and L-theanine, are gaining attention for their health benefits when inhaled through nebulization. L-theanine is known for promoting calmness and focus, offering a new way to enjoy tea's benefits beyond drinking it.

Puffpaw aims to replace nicotine and harmful chemicals with natural tea extracts, offering flavors that mimic traditional smoking. The goal is to make smoking a healthier daily habit. The first Puffpaw e-liquid pod, featuring these patented tea extracts, will launch alongside the Puffpaw Smart Vape (PSV).
